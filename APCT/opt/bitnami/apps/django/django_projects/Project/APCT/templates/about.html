Customer behavior monitoring is rapidly catching the eye of large chain retailers. How many customers are there in the store
at any given time and where they are spending their time is of interest for intelligent scheduling of employees and product displays.
As part of this project, you will implement a retail IoT solution which accomplishes the above mentioned goals using ceiling mounted
cameras. You will assume that cameras are mounted in each aisle. Your system will provide store owners following functionality:


  • Ability to create the store and its layout(asile and length of aisle)
  • Specify area of interest in a video stream coming from aisle video
  • Ability to IoT camera with specific aisle
  • View various statistics generated by camera measurements
    • Customer counts in entire store by time, day, week, and month
    • Customer counts in various aisles by time, day, week, and month
    • Time spent by customers at different locations in each aisle
